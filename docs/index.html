Gameplay

Explore. Befriend. Escape.

A full TUI game built for the terminal. Not a wall of text — styled panels, live status bars, progress indicators, and tab-autocomplete for every command, location, creature, and item.

Hit TAB and the terminal does the rest

01

Explore

Scan the terrain. Discover locations. Travel the ice — dodging geysers, storms, and crevasses.

02

Befriend

Talk to alien creatures with a local AI. Earn trust. Trade with merchants. Each has a role and a story.

03

Escape

Collect repair materials. Install them bay by bay. Fire the thrusters. Rise from the ice.

Systems

What Makes It Different

LLM Conversations

Talk to 10 alien archetypes with a local AI. Healers, Merchants, Warriors, Hermits — each with a personality and agenda. Every conversation is different.

Trade System

Merchants barter item-for-item. They never give for free. Bring what they want, and they have what you need.

Hostile Environment

Geyser eruptions. Ice storms. Crevasse falls. The environment is your enemy — and late-game weather makes it worse.

Escort & Repair

Befriend creatures and escort them to your ship. Builders install materials. Healers restore your vitals.

Procedural Worlds

Every game is different. Seeded RNG generates unique maps. Three modes from 30-minute sprints to 3-hour campaigns.

Works Offline

Local LLM inference. No internet. No cloud. Falls back to pre-written dialogue if no model installed.

Quick Start

macOS / Linux

Downloads the latest release binary. No Python required.

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/elephantatech/moon_traveler/main/install.sh | bash

Windows (PowerShell)

irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/elephantatech/moon_traveler/main/install.ps1 | iex
Or download a pre-built release directly →

AI Models (downloaded on first launch)

Choose one based on your available RAM. The game works without any model using pre-written dialogue.

SmolLM2 1.7B    1.0 GB download    ~1.2 GB RAM    (2-4 GB systems)
Qwen3.5 2B      1.3 GB download    ~2.3 GB RAM    (4+ GB, recommended)
Gemma 4 E2B     3.1 GB download    ~4.4 GB RAM    (8+ GB, best quality)
